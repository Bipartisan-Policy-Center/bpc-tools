⚠️ Phase-out Applies Your deduction is reduced because your income exceeds $150,000. The deduction phases out at a 10% rate above this threshold.
⚠️ Eligibility Check Required This deduction only applies to FLSA non-exempt workers. If you're salaried and exempt from overtime pay, you may not qualify even if you work extra hours.
💡 Important Notes:
  • This deduction expires December 31, 2028 (unless extended by Congress)
  • Federal payroll taxes (Social Security, Medicare) still apply to overtime wages
  • State income taxes may vary - check your state's rules
